Team-29: Assessment Investigator Project

Team-29 is composed of 3 Swinburne University Computer Science students who formed a team to work together on thier Major Capstone Project to create a software app designed to tackle the issue of contract cheating. The project was completely carried out online due to Covid restrictions and the major platform used for communications was Discord, whilst the versioning control system used was GitHub.
